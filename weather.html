Weather.

As our world is getting warmer every now and then. It is important for us to know the difference between weather and climate.

Weather Weather is the weather condition that is happening right now. And to happen soon, weather forecasters look at temperature, pressure, wind direction and humidity in a particular region and over a specific period of time. To know Will there be a lot of sunlight today? Will it rain tomorrow? And how likely it will snow, etc. In general, short-term weather forecasts are often quite accurate.

Weather Climate is the weather that occurs regularly for a long time. And rarely change much, such as polar climates Which is characterized by most cold weather The climate of each area is related to the different factors on Earth that cause different climatic features.

Scientists have observed changes in average temperature. To know the long-term trend of how the global climate is changing Which the current trend is clear that Our world is heating up. And it's having a big impact on all life on our planet. Causing extreme weather conditions Sea ice melt And rising sea levels, etc., which is a problem that affects all of us.
